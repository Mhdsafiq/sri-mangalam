Everything you need to know about our operations, security, and yield generation.

A chit fund is a savings and borrowing system where a group of members contribute a fixed amount monthly. Each month, the pooled amount is auctioned, and the lowest bidder wins. The difference (Kasar) is shared among all members, reducing their future instalments. Sri Mangalam operates with 20-member groups and ensures transparent, fair auctions.
We bring over 30 years of business experience. We offer fixed 10% Kasar without any foreman commission, easy documentation with no joining fee, multiple payment options (Bank Transfer, UPI, Cheque, App), and prize money disbursement within hours of the auction.
Absolutely. Sri Mangalam Chit Funds (Madurai) Pvt Ltd is a registered company operating strictly under the Chit Fund Act. We maintain complete transparency in all transactions, and all payments are processed exclusively through traceable bank transfers.
Any individual aged 18 and above with valid ID proof can join our schemes. Business establishments and organizations are also welcome to participate in our higher-tier plans ranging up to ₹50,00,000.
In each monthly auction, members who have not yet received the prize money can bid. The member who offers the highest discount (lowest bid) wins. The discount amount is distributed as Kasar (dividend) among all members. Sri Mangalam ensures fair and transparent auctions with a fixed 10% kasar.
