Our Legacy

From humble beginnings in 1942 to a multi-crore financial institution built on the foundation of trust.

A Legacy of Cultivation
Since 1942

Sri Mangalam Group’s legacy began in 1942 with M. Seeniyammaal’s cardamom cultivation in Munnar, Kerala, which expanded to over 100 acres under her family. Since then, the group has grown into a diversified business with strong roots in agriculture, trading, and hospitality, driven by quality and trust.

Est. 1995

Sri Mangalam Traders

An established wholesale commodity trader and distributor specialising in grain supply. Active in the regional market for nearly three decades, handling ₹30 crore in annual wholesale trade of sugar, rice, and pulses with over 1,000 loyal customers.

Est. 2005

Bracknell Forest Resort

Entered hospitality by providing premium and luxurious stays in the beautiful landscapes of Munnar.
