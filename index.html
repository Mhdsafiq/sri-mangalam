Our Legacy

A. Seenivasaganadar

Founder

1942: The Roots

Sri Mangalam Group's journey began with the vision of Mr. A. Seenivasaganadar, inspired by his mother M. Seeniyammaal's cardamom farming in Munnar in 1942.

1995: Business Expansion

Building on this legacy, he went on to establish Sri Mangalam Traders in Tirunelveli in 1995, a wholesale business dealing in sugar, rice, and pulses with over ₹30 crore in annual trade and 1,000+ loyal customers.

2020: Financial Evolution

In 2005, the group diversified into hospitality by launching Bracknell Forest Resort in Munnar. Continuing its legacy of growth, Sri Mangalam Chit Funds (Nellai) Pvt Ltd was founded in 2020, reflecting the group's commitment to innovation, trust, and community-focused expansion.
